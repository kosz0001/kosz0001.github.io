About Me

Hobbies

  • I enjoy collecting Pokemon cards
  • I like to watch True Crime documentaries

I'm a girl that loves any medium of art. I love music, I love watching movies, and I love traditional art (such as oil or watercolour paintings) but I also love digital art. This is ironic because I am not a good drawer in any capacity. In terms of music, I enjoy a wide variety. I have a tendency of liking certain songs but sometimes not liking the artist's entire repertoire. I do always joke with my friends that I have two moods for music: on the table dancing and crying. Quite the polar opposites, I know. I'm also a big Lord of the Rings fan. I recently bought the extended versions of The Hobbit and The Lord of the Rings on Apple TV so I have them on hand when I travel. I have them handy despite having seen the movies probably more times than is deemed healthy.

I am a huge fan of travelling. My last really big trip was before the pandemic in 2019 when I joined my cousin's backpacking trip in Asia. I was only able to stay for the leg of her trip that included Japan and South Korea. It was my 1st time flying over an ocean. I did go this past summer to San Diego on a solo trip and had the time of my life since there was a heat wave, and I'm tired of being cold. A year ago, I went with a friend to LA to see the Kpop group BTS. But honestly, the highlight of that trip was going to Disneyland and Universal Studios. My cousin and I are planning to go to New Zealand for a month after I graduate, and I'm excited to see all the Lord of the Rings filming locations. She's not a fan, but I hope she indulges me a bit.
