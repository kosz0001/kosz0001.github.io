About Me

I'm a girl that loves any medium of art. I love music, I love watching movies, and I love traditional art (such as oil or watercolour paintings) but I also love digital art, though I am not a good drawer in any capacity. In terms of music, I enjoy a wide variety. I have a tendency of liking certain songs but sometimes not liking the artist's entire repertoire.I do always joke with my friends that I have two moods for music: on the table dancing and crying. Two very opposite moods. I'm also a big Lord of the Rings fan. I recently bough the extended versions of The Hobbit and The Lord of the Rings on Apple TV so I have them on hand when I travel, despite having seen the movies probably way too many times.

I am a huge fan of travelling. My last really big trip was before the pandemic in 2019 when I joined my cousin's backpacking trip in Asia, but was only able to stay for the Japan and South Korean leg of her trip. It was my 1st time flying over an ocean. I did go this past summer to San Diego on a solo trip and had the time of my life since there was a heat wave. A year ago, I went with a friend to LA to see the Kpop group BTS. But honestly, the highlight of that trip was going to Disneyland and Universal Studios. My cousin and I are planning to go to New Zealand for a month after I graduate, and I'm excited to see all the Lord of the Rings filming locations. She's not a fan, but I hope she indulges me a bit.
